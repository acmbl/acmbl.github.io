Alexander Campbell

email: alexanderpcampbell@gmail.com

I am an Australian category theorist. My research interests are in category theory, especially higher and enriched category theory, and categorical aspects of homotopy theory.
I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Higher Categories and Categorification program at MSRI. From 2013 to 2019, I was a member of the Centre of Australian Category Theory at Macquarie University,
first as a PhD student and later as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow.
